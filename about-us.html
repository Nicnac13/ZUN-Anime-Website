Welcome to ZUN-Anime

We are the Zurich Universities Anime Association (ZUN-Anime for short), an association founded by students with the aim of promoting anime culture and bringing animanga fans together.
Founded in 2023, we currently have over 200 members on Discord. We create weekly 4-panel comics (drawn by LuluIcey), hold weekly animanga meetups where we host our own quizzes and other activities, as well as weekly art meetups.
We also offer art classes for artists of all levels. In addition to regular events, we also organise karaoke and have hosted our own maid café in the past.
No matter what you like, enter the realm of Animanga and meet other like-minded people.
Come and join us, either in person or on our Discord server!
